FISHING

Cucamonga-Guasti Park offers two lakes for year-round fishing Trout and Catfish.
A State of California fishing license is required to fish at Cucamonga-Guasti Regional Park. State licenses may be purchased on-line at the California Department of Fish and Game; https://www.wildlife.ca.gov/Licensing or at local sporting goods stores such as Bass Pro Shops or Big 5 Sporting Goods.
