Attractions

Music may be the heart of Huck Finn Jubilee, but we’re so much more than just a music festival. Our wide variety of attractions will allow you, your friends and family to enjoy activities that will stimulate your mind, body and soul. Here are just a few experiences that will be available to you in 2018!

The Huck Finn Jubilee Market Place will feature different types of food and craft vending. We are currently searching for the hippest, funkiest and most eclectic vendors in Southern California. Do you have a suggestion or know of a vendor that may be interested in being a part of the Huck Finn Jubilee family in 2018? We’ll be accepting vending applications starting in mid-late June on HuckFinn.com. If you have any questions, contact us at info@huckfinn.com!

We understand the immense value in being centered and relaxed. It can increase your focus, stamina, and overall wellbeing. We’re so excited to offer our guests a Yoga and Meditation Tent featuring daily sessions from a local Yoga studio.

Forget having to coordinate a child sitter, we encourage you bring the entire family to the 2018 Huck Finn Jubilee. We’re proud to be a festival that offers activities and fun for people of any age, especially the Lil’ Huckers (our punny name for kids that attend Huck Finn Jubilee). Such activities will include a Kid Zone, face painting, musical and educational activities for kids, an art station, a kids parade, and more to be announced! Please note that parental supervision is required at all times.

Part of our location’s charm is access to the lakes within the gorgeous city park. Fishing and Peddle Boating are returning to Huck Finn Jubilee! More details to come.

While on the topic of water activities, we’re so excited to bring you a Splash Pad for children of all ages. The Splash Pad is a zero depth water park featuring water-spraying nozzles in the shapes of mushrooms, flowers and other objects. This is going to be a great way for your children to enjoy a safe, fun way to enjoy the water!

In addition, we’re working hard to add several more actives to the roster. Stay tuned, right here, for more information to be continually added!
